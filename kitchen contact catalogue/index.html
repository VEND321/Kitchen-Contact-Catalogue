Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Can I book for multiple dates?

Yes, you can book items for multiple dates. Please specify each date when placing your order so we can check availability for all requested dates.

What's the minimum quantity?

We don't have a strict minimum quantity, but some items may have minimum rental requirements due to logistics. This will be communicated when you place your order.

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, we only offer pickup services. You'll need to arrange for pickup of your rented items at our location.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfers and mobile money payments. A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due before pickup.
