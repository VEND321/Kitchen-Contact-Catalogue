Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Can I book for multiple dates?

Yes, you can book items for multiple dates. Please specify each date when placing your order so we can check availability for all requested dates.

What's the minimum quantity?

We don't have a strict minimum quantity, but some items may have minimum rental requirements due to delivery logistics. This will be communicated when you place your order.

Is delivery included?

Delivery fees depend on your location and order size. We'll provide delivery cost estimates when you place your order.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfers and mobile money payments. A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due before delivery.
